01

Argan oil

Souss-Massa · Women's cooperatives

Cold-pressed from hand-cracked argan nuts by certified Berber cooperatives. Rich in vitamin E and oleic acid. Traceable batch number on every bottle.

02

Ghassoul clay

Atlas Mountains · Volcanic mineral deposit

Extracted from ancient volcanic deposits in the Atlas Mountains. Contains silica, magnesium, and potassium. A natural substitute for sulfate cleansers.

03

Prickly pear seed oil

Eastern Morocco · Cold-press extraction

110mg vitamin E per 100g — among the highest of any botanical oil. One metric ton of fruit yields one litre of oil. Used sparingly. Highly concentrated.

Our story

The hammam ritual has survived for centuries.
There is a reason.

Growing up in Morocco, the weekly hammam was not a beauty routine. It was a reset — a practice of cleansing, exfoliating, and nourishing the skin with the same botanical preparations used for generations.

HAYBA was built to bring that ritual into a modern D2C context — without losing the authenticity of its origins, and without demanding that every customer receive the same generic formula.
